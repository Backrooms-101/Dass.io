Blobfish Diet

A Blobfish is a Carnivores Animal(Organisms which only eat Other animals). The animals eaten by Blobfish are-Crabs,Lobsters,Sea urchins and Mollusks(SHOWN BELOW). The Blobfish have mastered the Ambush Predation to feed on them. Ambush Predation is a type of technique to capture their food by lying on the ocean floor and waiting for their prey to come near then and with a great surprise,the prey is being attacked by a great surprise.examples of Ambush Predation are Angle Shark fish,Blobfish etc.



Sea Urchins

Sea Urchins have a varity of colors as follows-black,red,brown,yellow,green and pink. Sea urchins are spiny marine creatures found globally. They possess a spherical body covered in movable spines and are echinoderms.


Mollusks

Mollusks are diverse invertebrates with soft bodies and hard shells, including snails, clams, squids, and octopuses, found worldwide.
